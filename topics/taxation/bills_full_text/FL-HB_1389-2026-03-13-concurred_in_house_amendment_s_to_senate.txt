A bill to be entitled

An act relating to affordable housing; amending ss.

125.01055 and 166.04151, F.S.; requiring counties and

municipalities, respectively, to authorize multifamily

and mixed-use residential uses as allowable uses for

specified parcels of land if certain conditions are

met; providing definitions; requiring counties and

municipalities, respectively, to eliminate parking

requirements for a proposed development under certain

circumstances; amending s. 196.1979, F.S.; authorizing

the board of county commissioners or the governing

body of a municipality to exempt specified portions of

property within multifamily projects and accessory

dwelling units that are used to provide affordable

housing; providing an effective date.
