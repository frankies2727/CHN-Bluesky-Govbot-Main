SYNOPSIS:

This bill would require high schools to allow

high school students participating in organized

National Signing Day activities related to the

acceptance of athletic scholarships, postsecondary

school educational commitments, apprenticeship

programs, and military enlistments to be excused from

class.

This bill would also require high schools to
provide reasonable accommodations for those activities.

A BILL

TO BE ENTITLED

AN ACT

Relating to public high schools; to require high

schools to excuse students from class to participate in

National Signing Day activities related to the acceptance of

athletic scholarships, postsecondary school educational

commitments, apprenticeship programs, and military

enlistments; and to provide reasonable accommodations for

those activities.

BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:

Section 1. (a) High school students participating
