Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Court assembled, and by the authority
of the same, as follows:

SECTION 1. Section 53 of Chapter 272 of the General Laws, as appearing in the 2022

Official Edition, is hereby amended by striking out, in lines 1-2, the words “Common night

walkers, common street walkers, both male and female,”.

following section:Section 34B: Any person who, in good faith, reports a crime shall not be charged or

prosecuted for (i) possession of a controlled substance under section 34, (ii) sex for fee under

subsection (a) and subsection (b) of section 53A of chapter 272, or found in violation of a

condition of probation or pretrial release as determined by a court or a condition of parole, as
