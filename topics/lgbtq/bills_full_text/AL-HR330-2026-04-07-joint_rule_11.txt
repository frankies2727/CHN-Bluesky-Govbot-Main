HR____ COMMENDING GRAYSON GAY FOR BEING NAMED A 2026

ALABAMA PUBLIC TELEVISION YOUNG HERO.

WHEREAS, highest commendations and congratulations

are proudly presented to Grayson Gay on being named a 2026

Alabama Public Television Young Hero; and

WHEREAS, Alabama Public Television selects Young

Heroes each year based on academic performance, service, and

resilience, highlighting students who have overcome

significant obstacles while continuing to contribute to

their schools and communities; each student receives a

scholarship and other prizes; and

WHEREAS, Grayson Gay is a junior at Cullman High

School who, after being diagnosed with stage three Hodgkin's

lymphoma in early 2025, stepped away from school activities

to undergo months of chemotherapy; and

WHEREAS, although cancer forced him to step away from

extracurricular activities and complete coursework

virtually, Grayson remained determined; throughout

treatment, he used social media to share encouragement and

faith-filled reflections, inspiring thousands in his

community; his message shifted from asking why he was facing

cancer to expressing gratitude for the strength it revealed

within him; his story resonated widely, drawing significant

community support and attention; and
Page 1

WHEREAS, now in remission, Grayson has returned to

campus and to the football field, regaining his strength and

preparing for the upcoming season; he adjusted his schedule

to include strength and conditioning to rebuild stamina and

position himself for a starting role; his oncology team has

been so inspired by his outlook that they have invited him

to mentor other children currently undergoing treatment in

the same cancer ward where he received care; and

WHEREAS, Grayson's servant heart extends throughout

his school and community; he is a member of Key Club and

Chick-fil-A Leader Academy; through these organizations, he

has helped organize service projects, including fundraising

initiatives and community meals for families in need; and

WHEREAS, Grayson maintains a 3.8 GPA while enrolled

in Advanced Placement and Dual Enrollment courses and is

preparing to take the ACT; despite the disruption of a

cancer diagnosis, he remains focused on his academic future

and aspires to pursue a career in pharmaceutical sales,

where he hopes to impact others in the health field; and

WHEREAS, Grayson Gay embodies resilience, faith, and

compassion; his courage in the face of illness, combined

with his commitment to serving others, makes him a powerful

example of strength and leadership; he is a deserving

recipient of recognition as a Young Hero; now therefore,

BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE

LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA, That Grayson Gay is honored and

commended, and this resolution is presented to him in

highest tribute and with heartiest congratulations, as well

as with sincere best wishes for continued success in all

future endeavors.
