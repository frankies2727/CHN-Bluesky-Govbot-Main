AN ACT
To amend chapter 188, RSMo, by adding thereto one new section relating to abortion.
Be it enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Missouri, as follows:

Section A. Chapter 188, RSMo, is amended by adding thereto

one new section, to be known as section 188.140, to read as

follows:

188.140.

1. As used in this section, the term "pro-

life individual or organization", shall mean any individual

or any nonprofit domestic firm, partnership, association, or

corporation, including those which operate under the

standards of section 501(c) of the Internal Revenue Code of

as amended, where the interests or purposes of such

individual or organization include, but shall not be limited

to, protecting the sanctity of life or defending the right

to life of all humans, born and unborn.

2.

Any pro-life individual or organization may

intervene in any action in which a provision of the

Constitution of Missouri, any Missouri statute, or any

Missouri rule that involves the regulation of abortion or

where the rights of an unborn child is at issue.

pro-life individual or organization to file for intervention

shall represent the rights of the unborn child in such

proceeding and shall have the right to file such pleading

necessary on behalf of the unborn child.
