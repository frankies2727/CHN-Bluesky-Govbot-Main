HOUSE BILL NO.

AN ACT TO AMEND SECTION 65-9-7, MISSISSIPPI CODE OF 1972, TO
REMOVE THE CAP ON THE NUMBER OF AUTOMOBILES THE DIVISION OF STATE
AID ROAD CONSTRUCTION IS AUTHORIZED TO OWN AND OPERATE IN ITS
FLEET; AND FOR RELATED PURPOSES.

BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI:

SECTION 1.

amended as follows:

65-9-7.

Section 65-9-7, Mississippi Code of 1972, is

The Division of State Aid Road Construction of the

Mississippi State Highway Department, is hereby authorized and

empowered to purchase, own and operate * * * the necessary number

of passenger automobiles deemed appropriate to carry out the

functions of the division, * * * in strict accordance with the

provisions of Sections 25-1-77 through 25-1-93 * * *.

SECTION 2.

This act shall take effect and be in force from

and after July 1, 2026.
