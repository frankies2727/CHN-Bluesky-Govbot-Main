SPONSOR: Rep. K. Johnson

HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
153rd GENERAL ASSEMBLY
HOUSE AMENDMENT NO. 1
TO
HOUSE BILL NO. 451

AMEND House Bill No. 451 on lines 18 and 19 by deleting “This section clarifies that liability under this chapter

may be established based on a practice’s discriminatory effect, regardless of discriminatory intent.” as it appears therein and

inserting in lieu thereof the following:

“This section clarifies and codifies the disparate impact framework recognized under the federal Fair Housing

Act, 42 U.S.C. §§ 3601 et seq., and liability under this chapter may be established based on a practice's discriminatory

effect.”.

FURTHER AMEND House Bill No. 451 on line 25 by deleting “(1)” as it appears therein.

FURTHER AMEND House Bill No. 451 by deleting lines 28 through 30 in their entirety.

FURTHER AMEND House Bill No. 451 by deleting lines 41 through 44 in their entirety and inserting in lieu

thereof the following:
“(1) This section applies to all discriminatory housing practices as defined under § 4602 of this title.”.
SYNOPSIS
House Amendment No. 1 to House Bill No. 451 removes the land-use provisions, algorithm-related language, and
enumerated covered practices.
