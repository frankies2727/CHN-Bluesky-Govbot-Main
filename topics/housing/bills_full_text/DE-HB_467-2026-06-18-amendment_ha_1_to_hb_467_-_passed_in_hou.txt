SPONSOR: Rep. Harris

HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
153rd GENERAL ASSEMBLY
HOUSE AMENDMENT NO. 1
TO
HOUSE BILL NO. 467

AMEND House Bill No. 467 on line 7 by deleting “agreement.” as it appears therein and inserting in lieu thereof
the following:

“agreement and may initiate coverage from a specific insurer or through a specific provider designated or affiliated

with the landlord if and only for as long as a tenant fails to initiate or maintain renters insurance required under the rental

agreement.”.
SYNOPSIS
House Amendment No. 1 to House Bill No. 467 clarifies that a landlord may initiate coverage from a specific
insurer or insurance provider designated or affiliated with the landlord if a tenant fails to initiate or maintain required
renters insurance and only as long as the tenant fails to initiate or maintain the required insurance.
